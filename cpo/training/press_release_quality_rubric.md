# Press Release Quality Rubric (1–5)

**5 — Excellent**: Specific, accurate, well-structured; clear headline; precise dates/locations; evidence and links present for claims; strong call-to-action (CTA); accessible (plain language), inclusive, and error-free.
**4 — Good**: Strong content with minor omissions or minor edits needed; evidence present where applicable; CTA present.
**3 — Fair**: Adequate but generic; missing some specifics; CTA or evidence may be weak or absent; some redundancy.
**2 — Poor**: Vague or disorganized; missing key details (dates/venues/methodology); no evidence for claims; tone inconsistent.
**1 — Very Poor**: Misleading or unsupported; missing core fields; factual or grammatical errors; no CTA when expected.
